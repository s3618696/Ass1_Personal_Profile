Project Ideas

Overview

Having two years of experience designing and developing mobile and web apps, I'm very excited to expand more on Artificial Intelligence and include this in my ideal project called Home Growth. The project will be an AI mobile app for growing food at home. This app acts like a farmer who will help the users detect plants, giving the user the appropriate conditions needed for the plant, push notifications for reminding details on taking care of their plants.


Motivation

As we all know, the world is suffering from the Corona Virus. Growing fresh vegetables at home are met by people around the world during the execution of a social blockade or social dilation to prevent the translation of the Covid-19 disease. According to Quang Nam Online Newspaper, "The consumption of seeds is increased globally. In Russia, the demand for seed increased by 20%-30% in March compared to the same period last year." To support the need for clean vegetable farming, I came up with this idea of doing this project to help people in productive plants, effectively, through which they would increase interest in agriculture, improve health, and protect the environment.


Description

The app will help users over three stages:

Stage 1: Preparation & Growing

・Users can search for a plant or take a picture of the plant they want to grow. The app will help detect the plant, and output to the user plant name, plant description, as well as the best environment to grow the plant.
・After users allow the app to access their location, the app will give the user advice on whether they should grow the vegetable depending on the location geography and weather.
・When users submit a growing plant, the app shows them the tree planting roadmap. The roadmap needs to be as detail as possible. Example: day 1 (water: 300ml, 30g Na....), day 2 (temperature: 26 degree,...).
・The app pushes the notification to remind watering, fertilizing

Stage 2: Growing and Harvesting

・Users take a picture of the plant, the app output the plant health condition depending on the color of the leaves, size of the plant,...
・The app gives users more tips to make the plant healthier or inform users to harvest if its good to do.
・Update the plant roadmap

Stage 3: After Harvesting

・The app gives users tips for recovering plants and provides a new roadmap to continue growing for the next harvesting.


Additional features during those stages:
・Users can be participating in the Home Growth Community for posting their plant processes.
・Users can trade their food with each other.


Tool & Technologies

This app development needs some reliable computer that can handle the neuron network's training model along with sufficient kernel on the virtual machine.


Skill Required

・Mobile Develop Skill (Flutter, React Native, Swift, Android)
・Deep Learning, Machine Learning
・Data Processing, Data Analysis.


Outcome

If the project is successful, it will be beneficial for everyone who loves planting and giving the most positive impact on reducing spreading Corona Virus. Moreover, clean food will help improve people's health and save the environment.
